Service FAQs

Is it safe for kids?

Yes! All activities have age restrictions for safety. Dolphin rides are suitable for all ages, while Jet Ski and Parasailing have minimum age limits of 12 and 10 years respectively. We prioritize safety with life jackets and professional supervision.

What should I wear?

Wear comfortable, quick-dry clothes or swimwear. Bring a change of clothes. We recommend avoiding loose items and bringing a towel. Sunscreen is highly recommended.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, free cancellation up to 2 hours before your scheduled time. Cancellations made less than 2 hours before will incur a 50% charge. No refund for no-shows.

Are there group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 4+ get 15% discount, and 9+ get 20% discount. Corporate and event packages available. Contact us for special pricing.
