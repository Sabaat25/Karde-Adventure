Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book an activity?

You can book through our website by clicking "Book Now", or contact us directly via phone, WhatsApp, or email. We'll confirm your booking within 2 hours.

What's the best time to visit?

October to May is the best season. October-November and February-March offer the most pleasant weather. Early morning (6-8 AM) is ideal for dolphin spotting.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 4-8 get 15% discount, and 9+ people get 20% discount. Contact us for corporate event packages and special pricing.

What's included in the package?

All packages include safety gear, life jacket, professional instructor guidance, and insurance. Some activities include photography options. Check service details for specifics.

How do I reach Karde Beach?

From Mumbai: ~220 km via NH48 and Sahyadri Express Way (4-5 hours). From Pune: ~110 km via NH65 (2-2.5 hours). Dapoli town is 15 km away with local transport available.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, free cancellation up to 2 hours before your scheduled time. Less than 2 hours notice incurs 50% charge. No-shows are non-refundable. Check our Terms & Conditions for details.
